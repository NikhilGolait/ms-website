About Maruti Suzuki

Overview

Maruti Suzuki India Limited is a leading automobile manufacturer in India, known for its innovative and reliable vehicles. As a subsidiary of the Japanese automaker Suzuki Motor Corporation, Maruti Suzuki has been a pioneer in the Indian automotive industry.

History

Founded in 1981, Maruti Suzuki has grown to become one of the most trusted car manufacturers in India. The company introduced its first car, the Maruti 800, in 1983, which revolutionized the Indian car market. Over the years, Maruti Suzuki has expanded its product line to include a wide range of vehicles, from compact cars to SUVs.

Key Achievements

  • Market leader in the Indian automotive industry for over three decades.
  • Known for producing fuel-efficient and affordable vehicles.
  • Strong focus on customer satisfaction and after-sales service.
  • Innovative technologies and environmentally friendly initiatives.
